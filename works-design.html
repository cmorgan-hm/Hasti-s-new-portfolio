design projects

The projector

printed advertisment - Tarjoman Magazine - Nov 2021

This poster, was designed to be published in a magazine for commercial purposes. However, the identity of the magazine demanded that I focus on the artistic qualities of the poster as well. In other words, this poster had to be more of an artwork than just an advertisement. 

The Iceberg

poster competition - Sep 2020

In the poster I designed, the heart floats like an iceberg the bulk of which is hidden under the water surface. Such is also the case with the tyrannical practice of live organ harvesting; people only see the tip of an iceberg and many are not aware of the full extent of the disaster caused by illegal harvesting in China and that’s why we should raise awareness around this subject.

Modern and Traditional

printed advertisment - Andishe Pouya Magazine - Dec 2021, Feb 2022

In this project I wanted to simulate the offline learning atmosphere of Khonyagar; so, by merging the guitar’s and setar’s (Iranian music instrument) necks’ and the progress bar of a video, I was able to depict that atmosphere. I wanted to show the variety of our educational field by using an electronic instrument and a traditional one.

How Does Falling in Love Feel Like

personal project - Jan 2021

In this artwork I tried to illustrate how I felt about love at the moment. I was fresh out of a break-up and I felt wounded and heartbroken. I pictured my feelings in this artwork. To me, love was like a beautiful flower growing inside my chest, running its roots deep inside my heart. However, with roots delving deeper and deeper came an agony which grew unbearable over time.
